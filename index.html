Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 22, 2025

Mouse Jiggler Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Browsing history

1.2 Data Stored Locally

  • Guest ID: Generated randomly and stored only on your device
  • Login Session: Local timestamp for session tracking
  • Bluetooth Connection: Device name and MAC address (local only)
  • App Preferences: Settings like sensitivity, scroll speed (local only)

1.3 Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

A. Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display banner advertisements
  • Data Collected: Device identifiers, IP address, app usage data
  • How it's used: To show relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

B. Unity Ads

  • Purpose: Display interstitial and rewarded video ads
  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, device info, app interactions
  • How it's used: Ad delivery and performance tracking
  • Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

2. Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
BLUETOOTH Connect phone as HID device to PC
BLUETOOTH_ADMIN Make phone discoverable for pairing
BLUETOOTH_CONNECT Maintain Bluetooth connection
BLUETOOTH_SCAN Scan for nearby Bluetooth devices (Android 12+)
INTERNET Load advertisements only (no other data sent)
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check internet connectivity for ads

3. How We Use Information

  • All user data (Guest ID, settings) stays on your device
  • No data is uploaded to our servers (we don't have any!)
  • Ad services collect anonymous usage data for ad personalization
  • Bluetooth data is used only for local device connection

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data. The only data sharing occurs with:

  • Ad Networks: AdMob and Unity Ads receive anonymous device identifiers for ad delivery
  • No third parties: We never sell or share your data with anyone else

5. Your Rights

  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Go to your device Settings ‚Üí Google ‚Üí Ads ‚Üí Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Delete data: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Access data: All data is stored in your device's app data folder

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally using Android's secure SharedPreferences
  • Bluetooth connections use standard encryption
  • No passwords or sensitive data are collected

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
